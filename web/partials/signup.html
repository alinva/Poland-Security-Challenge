Robert Kubica was kidnapped by a group of hackers. They have set up 3 security challenges on the way that leads to Robert Kubica.
But if the challenges will not be solved in 25 minutes - Robert will stay in danger and will not arrive to the "World Rally Championship"!!
Your goal is to save Robert Kubica by completing
the following 3 challenges:
1. Hack your way to start your car's engine.
2. Change the traffic light in your way.
3. Get through the parking lot's security check.


Good luck!
